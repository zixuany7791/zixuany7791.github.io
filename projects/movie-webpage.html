Movie Webpage Project

In this project, I used what I learned about HTML and CSS to create a movie poster called "Rick and Moron"

This movie is a comedy and sci-fi genre and two fictional character, rick and moron which rick is moron's grandfather from another dimension and traveled to here. Meanwhile Moron was diagnosed learning disability when he is borned. An adventure starts when Moron met Rick and wanted to follow him.

My partner and I worked together to make the movie webpage, each of us worked on different parts of coding. My partner work on the content of the story while I am trying to style the color of the background, the position of the image etc.

I think that the a challenge we faced was to put things at the place that we want, I tested a lot of things trying to figure out how to place images at the left / right side of the webpage and in the left / right side of the text. Another challenge we faced is to came up with a story of Rick and Moron. The story is close to the original Rick and Morty but it is not exactly the same like Moron follow Rick to his spaceship and left Earth without realizing he did. Throughout this project, I have learned how to self-study when making a project, communicating with my partner on how to split up the work. Without self-study, I wouldn't know how to put pictures on the left / right side. I also wouldn't know how to do linear-gradient.
